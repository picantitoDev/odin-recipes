Lemon Herb Chicken

This lemon-herb chicken is a simple, quick, and delicious dish.

All you need are a few herbs, a lemon, and of course, the chicken! The amount of spices is completely up to you.

Ingredients:

  • 2 (5 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 medium lemon, juiced, divided
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pinch dried oregano
  • 2 sprigs fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Steps:

  1. Place chicken in a bowl; pour 1/2 of the lemon juice over chicken and season with salt.
  2. Place chicken into hot oil and add the remaining lemon juice and oregano; season with black pepper.
  3. Cook chicken until golden brown and the juices run clear, 5 to 10 minutes per side.
  4. Garnish chicken with parsley to serve
